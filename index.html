Mad Lib Generator

Mad Libs are stories with certain types of words missing, which you fill in on your own; the results are supposed to be funny, but often just kind of stupid. Try putting in some of your own words (four per type of word, a comma between each word and NO SPACE). Choose a story for them to be plugged into, then just hit the Generate! button.
